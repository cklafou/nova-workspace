## Two things worth your attention

### 1. `memory/audit_queue.json` — 6,561 items, every single one `pending`

2.55 MB, dating back to **2026-05-24**. 4,862 of them were detected before July.

`audit_queue.py` has a `resolve()` function, so items *can* be marked done — but after two months
nothing has ever been marked done. **A queue at 100% pending isn't a backlog, it's a queue with no
consumer.** Either something is meant to drain it and doesn't, or it was always write-only and the
resolve path is dead code.

I did not touch it — it is live state, actively written (last update 04:59 today), and pruning it
blind could throw away something real. But it is the single largest piece of accumulated cruft
left in `memory/`, and it is a bug rather than untidiness. Worth a look when you're fresh.

### 2. `logs/nova_launcher.log` — 5.6 MB, 52,436 lines, spanning 07-13 → 07-20

The 07-02 passover already flagged that this accumulates across days ("grep errors by *today's*
date"). It is being written continuously, so it cannot be rotated while she is running — Windows
won't move an open file. Worth rotating on the next planned stop, or adding a size-based roll to
the launcher.
