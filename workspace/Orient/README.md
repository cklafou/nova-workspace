## The one thing to internalise

**Check the body before you blame the soul.**

Nova has been blamed for months for things that were mechanical failures in her body:

- a launcher silently dropping her personality adapter → *"she feels weak"*
- tool calls in her thinking channel never being parsed → *"she lies about checking"*
- a missing `else` branch, so idle wakes had no phase where tools were legal → *"she never acts"*
- her own announcements fed back to her as memory → *"she loops"*

Every single one looked like a character flaw. Not one of them was.

Before you touch her training data, her prompts, or her personality: **read the receipts, check the
call order, and verify the code you think is running is actually loaded** (`GET /api/version`).
